How can we help?

We help you get into Substance abuse and mental health treatment from certified professionals that offer various approaches to the recovery process, certified professionals in medication assisted treatment, CBT, DBT, 12 step recovery etc. We Specialize in assisting you with reintegrating yourself back into society and reconnecting yourself with loved ones. Our mission is to guide you into living the life you were meant to live.

Detox

Under the care of certified professionals, our detoxes in California will guide you on a restorative journey to cleanse your body, mind, and spirit.

Sober Living

Find your footing again with us - our sober living facilities are dedicated to easing your transition back into society by providing a supportive, nurturing environment.

Couples Welcome

Couples are warmly welcomed, our specialized marriage and relationship counselors are here to help reinforce and rebuild the bonds for supportive relationships in recovery.
